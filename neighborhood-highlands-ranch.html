Highlands Ranch, Colorado

Master-planned community with 70+ miles of trails, four rec centers, and consistent resale values across Douglas County.

About the Area

Why Families Choose Highlands Ranch

Highlands Ranch is one of the largest master-planned communities in the country, and there's a reason it keeps growing. Over 70 miles of paved trails connect the community to four full-service recreation centers, dozens of parks, and the Highlands Ranch Mansion event space. The infrastructure here was designed with families in mind from day one.

The housing stock spans a wide range — from townhomes in the low $400s to estate-style properties north of $1.5M. Most of the inventory falls between $550K and $850K, with strong resale numbers and consistent demand. Neighborhoods like Westridge, BackCountry, and Falcon Hills each have their own feel, lot sizes, and price bands.

Commute-wise, C-470 and I-25 are both accessible, and RTD's light rail extends to Lincoln Station on the northeast edge. Employers in the Denver Tech Center are a 10-15 minute drive. The trade-off is that some pockets of HR sit further south, which adds time if you're heading into downtown Denver regularly.

Lifestyle

What It's Like Living in Highlands Ranch

Key amenities and lifestyle highlights that define the area.

🚴

70+ Miles of Trails

Paved and soft-surface trails connecting parks, open space, and all four rec centers. The trail system is one of the most extensive in the metro area.

🏊

Four Rec Centers

Eastridge, Westridge, Southridge, and Northridge — each with pools, fitness, and programming. Residents get access to all four with an HRCA membership.

🏛

Highlands Ranch Mansion

A 9,200-square-foot historic estate that serves as an event venue and community gathering space. Hosts seasonal events year-round.

🚗

Commute Access

C-470 and I-25 provide direct routes to the DTC, downtown Denver, and DIA. Lincoln Station light rail connects to the E line.

🛒

Shopping & Dining

Town Center has a concentration of restaurants and retail. Nearby Park Meadows mall is one of the largest in the state.

⛰

Open Space

Over 2,000 acres of dedicated open space and 26 community parks. BackCountry Wilderness Area adds rugged terrain for hiking.
