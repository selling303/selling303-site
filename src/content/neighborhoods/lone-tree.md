aboutParagraphs:
  - "Lone Tree punches above its weight for a city of its size. It's home to Park Meadows — one of Colorado's largest shopping centers — the Charles Schwab campus, Sky Ridge Medical Center, and the growing RidgeGate mixed-use development. All of that sits within a few square miles, which creates a concentration of employment, retail, and services that most suburbs don't have."
  - "The housing market splits into two tiers. The Heritage Estates and Carriage Club neighborhoods offer estate-style homes on larger lots in the $900K-$2M+ range. Then you have RidgeGate's newer townhomes and paired homes that start in the low $500s, targeting younger professionals and downsizers. Median across the city runs around $725K, but your experience depends heavily on which pocket you're in."
  - "Lincoln Station, the RTD light rail stop on the E line, gives Lone Tree direct access to downtown Denver without getting on the highway. The RidgeGate development around the station is still building out, adding restaurants, offices, and housing that will change the area's density over the next few years. For buyers who want newer construction with good transit access in Douglas County, this is where the action is."
