The Short Version

Real Estate as a Financial Decision

I got into real estate because I saw too many families making one of the biggest financial decisions of their lives based on gut feelings and generic advice. That didn't sit right.

My approach is different. I treat every transaction like an investment — because it is one. Before recommending a price, I look at absorption rates, DOM trends, and actual comp data. Before writing an offer, I model the numbers and pressure-test the assumptions.

I specialize in the South Denver Metro — Littleton, Highlands Ranch, Centennial, Englewood, Parker, Lakewood, Lone Tree, Castle Pines, and Greenwood Village. I know these neighborhoods at the HOA level, the street level, and the data level. That specificity matters when you're making a $600K+ decision.

Whether you're a growing family looking to move up, a seller trying to maximize net proceeds, or an investor evaluating a deal — I bring the same process: clear data, honest context, and a plan that actually makes sense for your situation.
