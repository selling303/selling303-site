About Jacob

Meet Jacob Stark

REALTOR® at 8z Real Estate · Your Guide Through South Denver Metro Real Estate

The Short Version

Real Estate Done Differently

I got into real estate because I watched too many families make one of the biggest financial decisions of their lives without a real guide in their corner. Generic advice, vague timelines, pressure to decide fast. That didn't sit right.

Before I became a REALTOR®, I was a marketer. I flipped houses. I learned what it feels like to be on the other side of the table — and I decided that if I was going to do this job, I was going to do it differently. Every decision backed by real data, every update delivered clearly, and every client treated like someone whose future actually matters.

I specialize in the South Denver Metro — Littleton, Highlands Ranch, Centennial, Englewood, Parker, Lakewood, Lone Tree, Castle Pines, and Greenwood Village. I know these neighborhoods at the HOA level, the street level, and the comps level. That specificity matters when you're making a $600K+ decision.

Whether you're a growing family looking to move up, a seller trying to maximize your net, or an investor running deal numbers — you'll get the same thing from me: honest context, a clear strategy, and someone who actually picks up the phone.
