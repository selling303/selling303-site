Our South Denver Service Area

Jacob Stark serves buyers and sellers across nine South Denver Metro communities — from Jefferson County in the west to Douglas County in the south, and Arapahoe County in between.

Littleton
Arapahoe & Jefferson County. Along the South Platte River corridor, C-470 and US-85.
Highlands Ranch
Douglas County. South of Lone Tree, anchored by the HRCA rec centers and E-470.
Centennial
Arapahoe County. Bordered by Greenwood Village (north), Parker (east), and Highlands Ranch (south).
Englewood
Arapahoe County. Immediately south of Denver, close-in with light rail running through Broadway.
Parker
Douglas County. Southeast of the metro along E-470, adjacent to Castle Rock.
Lakewood
Jefferson County. West side of the metro, bordering Denver and running up to the foothills near Red Rocks.
Lone Tree
Douglas County. Just north of Highlands Ranch, anchored by Park Meadows and Lincoln Station light rail.
Castle Pines
Douglas County. South of Lone Tree, east of Castle Rock, surrounded by the namesake pine forests.
Greenwood Village
Arapahoe County. Sits inside the Denver Tech Center, bordered by Centennial, Englewood, and Cherry Hills Village.
